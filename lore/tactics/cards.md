# Tactic Cards — The Dice-Combat Layer

### *IMPERIUM: Twilight of Empires* (1400–1453)

Twenty-four tactic cards for the combat layer, in three tiers. Commons are small,
reliable edges; uncommons are situational swings; rares are the moments a chronicle
remembers. Card slugs are stable identifiers and must not change once shipped.

Every mechanical effect below is a **writing-side proposal** phrased in the working
design vocabulary (dice, rerolls, the wall bonus, siege attrition, rounds). None of it
is ratified. Flavor text follows `lore/STYLE.md`; effect lines follow UI conventions
(bare numerals, imperative present).

## Common

### `forced-march`
**Forced March**
*The drums sounded before dawn, and the column ate two days' road in one.*
**Effect:** One of your hosts moves 1 additional province this turn; it may not lay siege on arrival. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `veterans-of-the-border`
**Veterans of the Border**
*They had fought on this ground before, and the ground remembered them.*
**Effect:** Add 1 die to one of your hosts in a single land battle. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `pilot-of-the-narrows`
**The Pilot of the Narrows**
*He had run the strait in fog and in dark, and knew where the current turned.*
**Effect:** Add 1 die in a single fleet battle. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `ladders-and-fascines`
**Ladders and Fascines**
*The ditch was filled by night; by morning the ladders stood against the sky.*
**Effect:** Reroll one of your dice in a single siege assault round. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `the-counting-house`
**A Good Season at the Counting-House**
*The ledgers closed fat that year, and the factor permitted himself one smile.*
**Effect:** Gain 2 Gold when played. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `grain-barges-of-the-danube`
**Grain Barges of the Danube**
*The barges rode low in the water, heavy with the harvest of the plain.*
**Effect:** Gain 2 Grain when played. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `ears-in-the-bazaar`
**Ears in the Bazaar**
*Every price in the market was known to him, and some of those prices were men.*
**Effect:** Look at all tactic cards held by one rival. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `locked-shields`
**Locked Shields**
*The line bent, and held, and the charge broke on it like water on stone.*
**Effect:** When one of your hosts defends in a land battle, reroll your lowest die. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

## Uncommon

### `feigned-retreat`
**Feigned Retreat**
*They fled just badly enough to be believed.*
**Effect:** In one land battle, before hits are dealt, withdraw your host in good order to an adjacent province you control; the battle ends. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `night-sortie`
**Night Sortie**
*The gate opened without a torch, and the besiegers' fires went out one by one.*
**Effect:** For one round, siege attrition falls upon the besieger instead of the besieged. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `bribed-gatekeeper`
**The Bribed Gatekeeper**
*His price was forty ducats and a fast horse. The wall's price was higher.*
**Effect:** Cancel the wall bonus for one round of a siege you are pressing. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `chain-across-the-horn`
**The Chain Across the Horn**
*Iron lay across the water, and the whole sea stopped to consider it.*
**Effect:** One port province you hold cannot be attacked by sea until the start of your next turn. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `condottieri-contract`
**Condottieri Contract**
*He read the contract twice, signed it once, and fought precisely as long as he was paid.*
**Effect:** Spend 2 Gold: add 2 dice to one of your hosts in a single land battle. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `papal-indulgence`
**Papal Indulgence**
*Grace, the legate explained, had a schedule of fees.*
**Effect:** Spend 2 Gold: gain 3 Faith. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `the-intercepted-letter`
**The Intercepted Letter**
*The courier reached the wrong camp — which was, for somebody, the right one.*
**Effect:** Cancel a tactic card as a rival plays it; discard both cards. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `the-hexamilion-manned`
**The Hexamilion Manned**
*Six miles of stone across the Isthmus, and for one night every yard of it awake.*
**Effect:** One of your hosts defending a province gains the wall bonus for a single land battle. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

## Rare

### `greek-fire`
**Greek Fire**
*The sea itself burned, and water would not put it out.*
**Effect:** Win one fleet battle outright, before any dice are cast; then discard this card. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `master-founders-hired`
**Master Founders Hired**
*Men who cast thunder in bronze did not come cheap; the treasury paid, and did not haggle.*
**Effect:** In one siege, cancel the wall bonus for one full round and add 1 die to your assault. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `treason-at-the-gate`
**Treason at the Gate**
*The Kerkoporta stood open a hand's breadth, and an empire passed through it.*
**Effect:** End a siege you have pressed for at least 2 rounds: the province falls without a final assault; then discard this card. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `the-pay-chest-taken`
**The Pay Chest Taken**
*The escort was loyal; the road was long; the chest was very heavy.*
**Effect:** Take 3 Gold from one rival's treasury and add it to your own. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `holy-war-proclaimed`
**Holy War Proclaimed**
*Rome preached the cross, the Porte proclaimed the ghaza, and Heaven was claimed by every banner in the field.*
**Effect:** Spend 2 Faith: all your hosts add 1 die in every battle until the start of your next turn. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `sails-from-the-west`
**Sails from the West**
*Four hulls against a hundred, and the wind, for once, chose the four.*
**Effect:** Play when a port province you hold is under siege: siege attrition ceases this round, and you gain 2 Grain. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `a-death-in-the-palace`
**A Death in the Palace**
*The drums fell silent at Adrianople, and every war paused to count the heirs.*
**Effect:** Name one rival: a truce holds between you until the start of your next turn; no new battles or sieges may begin between your forces and theirs. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

### `the-white-knights-stroke`
**The White Knight's Stroke**
*Where his banner turned, the battle turned with it.*
**Effect:** In one land battle, reroll all of your dice once; keep the second result. *(proposed — design to ratify)*

## Distribution notes

Proposed deck construction for the tactics deck:

| Tier | Cards | Copies each | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| Common | 8 | 3 | 24 |
| Uncommon | 8 | 2 | 16 |
| Rare | 8 | 1 | 8 |
| **Deck** | **24** | — | **48** |

Rares appear once each, so their dramatic effects are once-per-game by construction;
the two that also say "discard this card" (`greek-fire`, `treason-at-the-gate`) are
meant to leave the game entirely rather than return with a reshuffle.

All counts, costs, and magnitudes above are **proposed for design to tune** — copy
counts, deck size, and whether the discard pile reshuffles are open questions for the
designers, not commitments of this document.
