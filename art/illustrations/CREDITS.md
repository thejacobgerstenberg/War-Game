# Art Credits — IMPERIUM: Twilight of Empires

All vignettes under `art/illustrations/` are **original vector work** created for
*IMPERIUM: Twilight of Empires*. No external assets, traces, stock art, clip art,
or third-party sources of any kind were used — every path was drawn from scratch
as hand-written SVG.

## Directories

- **`events/`** — card-bound art for the Omen Deck (`docs/EVENT_CARDS.md`); see
  [`events/MANIFEST.md`](events/MANIFEST.md) for the slug → card binding.
- **`lore/`** — chronicle / finale / tutorial vignettes drawn from
  `lore/events/flavor.md` that bind to no event card.
